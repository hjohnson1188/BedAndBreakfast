Since 1979, the Eau Claire Figure Skating Club has been serving the community by providing skating instruction, hosting skating competitions and the yearly skating show and offering various club activities to both the young and old in Western Wisconsin.


The Eau Claire Figure Skating Club is a proud partner with US Figure Skating, The Professional Skaters Association and Learn To Skate USA.
The Learn To Skate USA program is in collaboration with US Hockey, US Speed Skating and US Figure Skating.

Every year, in Novemeber and March, the Eau Claire Figure Skating Club hows their annual competitions. Basic Skills in November and Non-Qualifying in March. At the March competition there is also, the annual Synchronized Skating Competition. Every year, in April, the Eau Claire Figure Skating Club hosts their Annual Ice Show.
